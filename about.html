About

My passion is storytelling through video. I use media, graphics, and color to capture beautiful and meaningful moments. Video is a powerful medium with the capacity to tell an engaging story, taking the first layer of emotion and elevating it with visual and auditory elements, from polaroid graphics that capture a memory to videos of your loved ones singing and dancing. My montages celebrate pivotal milestones, such as bar and bat mitzvahs, wedding anniversaries, celebrations of life, and even birthdays. I'm excited to work with you to create a memorable montage that you will treasure for years to come.
